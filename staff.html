Meet Our Coaching Staff

Our coaches are dedicated to helping players achieve their highest potential, with specialized training and international qualifications.

Ashwin Kumar Padukone

With over a decade of experience in coaching and a passion for table tennis, Coach Ashwin has guided numerous players to success at regional and national levels. His expertise and dedication have made PTTCE a prominent center for table tennis training excellence.

Coach Rohan Desai

Assistant Coach
